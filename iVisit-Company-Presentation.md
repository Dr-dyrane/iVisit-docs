iVisit is a next‑generation emergency medical dispatch and coordination platform designed to function as **lifeline infrastructure** for Lagos and emerging nations. Built with Apple‑level design discipline and Google‑grade engineering rigor, iVisit unifies patients, ambulances, and hospitals into a single, real‑time ecosystem.

Every second matters in emergency care. iVisit ensures those seconds are never wasted.

### **Mission Statement**  
*"To revolutionize emergency medical care access by providing ultra‑rapid dispatch and real‑time coordination between patients, providers, and emergency responders."*

### **The iVisit Promise**  
**Immediate Access. Coordinated Care. Every Visit Matters.**

iVisit bridges the gap between fragmented healthcare systems and patients in urgent need. Through real‑time coordination, unified data, and intelligent dispatch, iVisit transforms emergency response from a slow, unpredictable process into a precise, orchestrated system.

# **2. The Problem**

Emergency medical systems in developing nations face structural challenges:

- **8–12 minute average response times** in urban centers  
- **Fragmented communication** between hospitals, ambulances, and patients  
- **Siloed medical data** across incompatible systems  
- **Underutilized resources** due to lack of real‑time visibility  
- **No unified command center** for emergency operations  

These inefficiencies cost lives every day.

iVisit delivers a fully integrated emergency response ecosystem:

### **Ultra‑Rapid Dispatch**

# **4. Core Doctrine: Unity Architecture**

At the heart of iVisit is a simple but powerful doctrine:

> **“Everything else exists inside, after, or because of these two actions.”**

### **Primary Action — Emergency Ambulance Dispatch**  
Demand generation and immediate response coordination  
- SOS activation  
- GPS location broadcasting  
- Nearest‑unit dispatch  
- Live routing and arrival management  

### **Secondary Action — Hospital Bed Booking & Capacity Management**  
Resource allocation and asset management  
- Real‑time bed availability  
- Reservation workflows  
- Patient intake coordination  
- Capacity forecasting  

These two actions define the entire emergency care lifecycle.  
iVisit unifies them into one seamless system.
